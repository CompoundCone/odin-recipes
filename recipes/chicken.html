Chicken

Description

This is an easy but boring recipe for grilled chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cracked black pepper if your feeling adventurous

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil over medium heat
  2. Add salt to chicken breast
  3. Add chicken breast, flipping occasionally. Cook to 155 F
  4. Hit it with some black pepper
  5. Enjoy
