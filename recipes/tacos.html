Tacos

Description

This is a tasty recipe for tacos utilizing carne asada, cilantro, fresh lime, and picked radish It is quick to make and has many variations.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Carne Asada
  • Cilantro
  • Fresh Limes
  • Pickled Radish
  • Your Favorite Hot Sauce (cholula)

Steps

  1. Grill the carne asada to medium rare
  2. Chop the cilantro, quarter the limes
  3. Add corn tortillas to the grill until slightly charred
  4. Add asada and radish to tortilla. Top with cilantro, hot sauce, lime juice
  5. Enjoy
