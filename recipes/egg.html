Egg

Description

This is a tried and true, never fail, recipe for perfect slightly runny boiled eggs

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ice
  • Copious amounts of black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add eggs for exactly 8 minutes
  3. Make bowl of ice water
  4. Cool eggs in ice water for 2 minutes
  5. Peel
  6. Enjoy perfect semi-runny eggs
  7. Have small emotional breakdown when you peel eggs and they are completly undercooked even though you have used this cooking time several times in the past and it always worked but for some reason this time it barely cooked the eggs and now you dont have anything to eat but eggy soup :(
